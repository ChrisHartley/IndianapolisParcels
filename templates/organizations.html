Third Party Buyers

Renew Indianapolis allows third-party application submissions, provided the applicant who will take title to the property provides authorization. In these cases, the third-party should complete the application in his/her account, but note the affiliated entity or individual on the application.

Examples of this include a Realtor applying on behalf of a client, a family member or friend completing an application on behalf of another person, an employee applying for a company, or an owner applying for a property that will be owned through a self-directed IRA.

The entities or individuals that have provided authorization allowing you to submit applications on their behalf appear on this page. You can also add new entities or individuals on this page.

If you are applying for a property that you will personally own, then you don't need to do anything here.
