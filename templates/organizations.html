Third Party Buyers

If you are applying for a property on behalf of another person, a company or other entity that will take title to the property then they must be listed on your application as the third party buyer.

Examples of this include a Realtor applying on behalf of a client, an employee applying for a company or an owner applying for a property that will be owned through a self directed IRA.

You can view your existing third party buyers or add a new one on this page. If you are applying for a property for yourself then you don't need to do anything here.
