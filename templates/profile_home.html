Thank you for your interest in Renew Indianapolis and our properties.

Renew Indianapolis exists to return chronically vacant and abandoned property to productive use. We accept donated properties and work with the City of Indianapolis on select city-owned properties. View our complete, up to date, inventory on our property map.

Our online application is free, with no application fee, and we welcome interested buyers of all types—individuals, non-profit organizations, and for-profit corporations.

Get started on an application by clicking "Start a New Application" or resume work on an existing application by clicking "edit" next to the application.

We consider applications on a rolling basis. Our monthly deadline is typically the 2nd Friday of the month (3rd Friday of the month when a month contains 5 Thursdays). Applications seeking to rehabilitate an existing structure or to construct a new structure require a scope of work, outlining the improvements to be made to the site, and proof of funds, demonstrating the capacity to complete the proposed scope of work.

Request a visit to a property to prepare your scope of work by clicking the "Request A Property Visit" button.

For more information, please see our policies and proceedures page.
